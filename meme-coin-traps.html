How to Avoid Meme Coin Traps (Even as a Degen)

Solana is the land of opportunity — and also the land of traps. If you’ve ever bought a coin and watched it nuke before your transaction confirmed, this one’s for you.

🧠 Key Red Flags to Watch For:

  • Locked TG – Devs don’t want real community questions.
  • Hidden Links – No social links or a random Linktree full of junk.
  • No Wallets Shown – You can’t verify top holders = big risk.
  • Dev Wallet Selling – If the dev wallet sells early, game over.
  • Fake Volume – 50+ txs in a row with tiny amounts? It’s likely spoofed.

🔍 Tools to Help You DYOR Smarter:

  • Birdeye – Track volume, holders, dev sells.
  • Dexscreener – Set alerts, check liquidity and chart strength.
  • RugCheck – Instant audit scores for new launches.
  • HowRare – For NFT-backed tokens or Solana NFTs.

💡 Extra Pro Tips

  • Look at volume spikes — if they don’t match the chart, something’s off.
  • Check Telegram activity — too quiet or too spammy = red flag.
  • Follow smart wallets — if they’re buying, it might be real.
